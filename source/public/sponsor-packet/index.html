    Boise Code Camp Is Back! Don't miss your chance to get Involved!

    Boise Code Camp was started 2004 and has run consistently minus a few years due to Covid! We are ecstatic to be back in the scene of live training events.

    Saturday May 4, 2024 

    Welcome Code Camp Supporters

    Thank you for supporting the developer community over the past 20 years. Boise Code Camp is a free one-day event full of presentations for developers, hardware geeks, and the curious of all ages and skills to come and learn. This event is presented in partnership with CodeWorks a software developer training company.

    Our organization, BoiseCodeCamp, is an Idaho non-profit. We only exist through the generosity of our volunteers and our sponsors each year. 

    We are excited to present our plans for Boise Code Camp this year, and with the help of your valued contribution we are aiming to raise a total of  $10,000 to fulfill our mission of elevating the awareness, education and overall technical skill of our community. 


    Why Boise Code Camp

    1) 20 Years of Community! We know who you need.

    2) Incredible registration and attendance This is where you come to find the hungry and excited talent

    3) Outstanding Guest Speakers from some of the largest tech companies in the world.

    4) Have we mentioned it is free to attend.

    5) The best place to geek out with fellow nerds.

    Who attends Code Camp?

    BOISE'S hidden trove of talent does

    Over the past 20 years we have built and maintained an incredible tech community with over 1,100 members. Through consistent efforts of user groups like Boise Software Developers Group (BSDG) companies like CodeWorks and the undying efforts of community leaders such as Scott Nichols, Chris Brandsma, Andrew Hanson, Brian Lagunas we know how to source and grow the skills needed for today's tech demands.

    Year after year as we gather we speak geek and keep our skills sharp! We take special care to learn what this talent is looking for in their careers and we make lasting connections. We know our community members and you can leverage these crucial connections are crucial when looking for that next hire.

    Sponsorship brings new Tech Talent

    The Benefits

    In the 21st century, Idaho needs software innovators and we have a responsibility to invest in the next generation of software developers. Software development is challenging and creative and it thrives in a vibrant community. Since 2004, Boise Code Camp has been building that community and we need your financial support. Our event has always been free to anyone interested in learning about technology and connecting with others. Please join us in supporting the software developer community in Idaho.

    BoiseCodeCamp is an Idaho NonProfit. This means your sponsorship is a tax deductible donation. The organizers, volunteers, presenters and sponsors are dedicated to the mission of providing the highest quality content built around the topic of software development and toward creating an environment where knowledge is easily and freely shared.
